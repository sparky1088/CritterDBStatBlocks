Stone Strix

Huge monstrosity, unaligned

Armor Class

16 (natural armor)

Hit Points

147 (14d12 + 56)

Speed

20 ft., fly 60 ft.

Saving Throws

Dex +6, Con +7

Skills

Perception +8

Senses

darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 18

Languages

—

Challenge

8 (3900 XP)

Flyby.

The stone strix doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach.

Keen Hearing and Sight.

The stone strix has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight.

Petrifying Gaze.

If a creature starts its turn within 30 feet of the stone strix and the two of them can see each other, the stone strix can force the creature to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw if the stone strix isn’t incapacitated. On a failed save, the creature magically begins to turn to stone and is restrained. It must repeat the saving throw at the end of its next turn. On a success, the effect ends. On a failure, the creature is petrified until freed by the greater restoration spell or other magic.
 A creature that isn’t surprised can avert its eyes to avoid the saving throw at the start of its turn. If it does so, it can’t see the stone strix until the start of its next turn, when it can avert its eyes again. If it looks at the stone strix in the meantime, it must immediately make the save.
 If the stone strix sees its reflection within 30 feet of it in bright light, it mistakes itself for a rival and targets itself with its gaze.

Actions

Multiattack.

The strix makes two attacks: one with its beak and one with its talons.

Beak.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 22 (4d8 + 4) piercing damage.

Talons.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 18 (4d6 + 4) slashing damage.
